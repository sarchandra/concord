K.T. Ravindranath

Founder President

K.T. Ravindranath founded Concord International, which was incorporated in 1973. Ravindranath was born in Kerala on 1st January, 1941. He attended Municipal High School, Kannur, after which he completed BSc Engineering (Electrical) from College of Engineering, Thiruvananthapuram in 1965. During his time at College of Engineering, he was actively involved in NCC. After spending a few years working for Toshniwal Bros (Pvt.Ltd), Chennai, in 1973, he went on to start Concord International based in Bangalore. He was a self-made, self-driven and motivated entrepreneur and a visionary who took firm, decisive decisions which spearheaded Concord International to its current success.
