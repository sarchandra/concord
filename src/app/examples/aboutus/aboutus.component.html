About Concord

CONCORD provides Instrumentation services to the various sectors of Indian Industry. The company is engaged in the marketing of Test and Measuring equipments and represents several leading Principals based in Europe and USA, exclusively in India. The company has long standing association with major Industrial and Defence Establishments in India, including:
  • Automotive Industries
  • Government Research and Development Establishments
  • Public sector and Private sector Industries
  • Defence Research Development Organisation
  • Indian Aerospace Industry
  • Department of Space
A close working relationship with our Principals along with the Customers is an important strategy that has allowed the company to establish firmly in the Indian Industry. This co-operation has promoted the development of mutually beneficial solutions resulting in the delivery of timely and cost effective critical instrumentation projects all over India.
Concord provides services like installation and commissioning, after sales service, as well as a wide range of managerial, technical and commercial skills during the execution of projects across the country.
